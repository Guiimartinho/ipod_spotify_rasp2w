# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

This is a small, friendly hobby project. We want participation in sPot — issues, pull requests,
and discussions — to be a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of
background or experience level.

## Our standards

We ask everyone to:

- Be respectful, patient, and considerate.
- Assume good faith and offer constructive feedback.
- Keep discussion focused on the project and its technical merits.
- Respect differing viewpoints and decisions made by the maintainers.

Behavior that is disrespectful, harassing, or otherwise harmful to others is not welcome.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull requests, and discussions — and
when representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately to the maintainer
[@Guiimartinho](https://github.com/Guiimartinho). Reports will be reviewed and handled with
discretion. Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions, comments, and other interactions
that do not align with this Code of Conduct, and may block repeat or serious offenders.
